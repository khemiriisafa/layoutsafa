Today, lattes are popular in coffee shops all over the world. Many people even make them at home with specialty coffee machines. Lattes are easy to make. All you need to do is fill an eight-ounce cup with either a single (one-ounce) or double (two-ounce) shot of espresso and steamed milk, leaving enough room for a thin, half-inch layer of foamed milk on the top.
